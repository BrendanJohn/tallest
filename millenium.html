MILLENNIUM TOWER

1 Franklin Street

The newest tall tower to hit the Boston area, this impressive structure features luxury apartments and condominiums that as well as retails space including a supermarket and additional office space. Completed in 2016, this ambitious structure commands the downtown Boston skyline. Containing over 400 luxury condos in its impressive glass facade, the building is striking and a candidate for the most magnificent tall tower in Boston area.
